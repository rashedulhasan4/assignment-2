Standard Curriclam

Kinder (3-6) Years

Our curriculum for children aged 3 to 6 years focuses on nurturing curiosity, developing social skills, and building a strong foundation in early literacy and numeracy. Through play-based learning and hands-on activities, children discover the joy of exploration and creativity in a safe and supportive environment.

Read More

Elementary School

Our Elementary School program is designed to inspire a love for learning and foster intellectual growth. Students are engaged in a variety of subjects including math, science, language arts, and social studies. With a mix of interactive lessons, group projects, and individual assignments.

Read More

Middle (10-16) Years

Our curriculum for ages 10 to 16 focuses on providing a comprehensive education that prepares students for higher education and future careers. With an emphasis on core subjects, as well as elective courses in the arts, technology, and physical education, students develop a well-rounded skill set.
